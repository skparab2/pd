PD Tests - About

These tests are being conducted by Shubham Parab, a high school researcher, and Dr. Peter Washington, Assistant professor of Computer Science at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. The goal of these tests is to eventially create a means of accessible, easy and free deagnisis for Parkinson's Disease. To do this, both participants suffering from the disease and healthy participants are being asked to complete this series of tests, which will include moving a mouse in a specified direction, and tapping specific keys when prompted. If there are any questions about this, you can reach out at skparab1@gmail.com. Thank you!
